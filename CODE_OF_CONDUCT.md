# Code of Conduct

To maintain a positive and inclusive environment, all contributors are expected to follow these rules:

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and supportive toward others.
- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Provide helpful and constructive feedback.
- Collaborate with patience and kindness.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech.
- Personal attacks or insults.
- Sharing private or sensitive information.
- Any actions that make others feel unsafe.

## Enforcement
Violations may result in warnings or removal from the project. Serious cases may be reported to higher authorities.

## Thank You
By contributing to this project, you help create a safe and collaborative environment for everyone.
